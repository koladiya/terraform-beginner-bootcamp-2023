Dabeli Recipe

Recipe

This is a simple and delicious Dabeli recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 Pav (Indian bread rolls)
  • 4-5 medium-sized boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 2 tablespoons Dabeli masala
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind chutney
  • 2 tablespoons garlic chutney
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add Dabeli masala. Saut√© for a minute.
  2. Add mashed potatoes and salt. Mix well.
  3. Split the pav into half and lightly roast them.
  4. Spread garlic chutney and tamarind chutney on the pav.
  5. Fill the pav with the potato mixture.
  6. Garnish with roasted peanuts and pomegranate seeds.
